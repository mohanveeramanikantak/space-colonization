# Space Colonization – Detailed Notes

## 1. Introduction
Space Colonization is the idea of humans living permanently outside Earth using advanced technologies and sustainable ecosystems.

---

## 2. Why Space Colonization?
- Overpopulation on Earth
- Resource scarcity
- Scientific exploration
- Long-term survival of humanity

---

## 3. Potential Locations

### 3.1 Moon
Closest celestial body suitable for research bases.

### 3.2 Mars
Most promising planet for human settlement.

### 3.3 Space Stations
Artificial habitats orbiting planets.
