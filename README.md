# 🚀 Space Colonization

## 📌 Overview

Space Colonization refers to the concept of establishing permanent human settlements beyond Earth, including the Moon, Mars, and other celestial bodies.

This field combines space science, robotics, artificial intelligence, biotechnology, and advanced engineering to enable long-term survival in space.

---

## 🔮 Future Vision

* Human settlements on Mars
* Lunar research bases
* Space agriculture systems
* Interplanetary transportation
* Self-sustaining space habitats

---

## ⚙️ Core Technologies

### 1. Space Robotics

Used for exploration, construction, and maintenance.

### 2. Artificial Intelligence

Helps in autonomous decision-making and mission planning.

### 3. Life Support Systems

Maintain oxygen, water, and food supply.

### 4. Space Transportation

Advanced spacecraft for interplanetary travel.
